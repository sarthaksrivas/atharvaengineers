About Us

In the year 1991 an enterprising and Entrepreneurial engineer and Founder Shri Pramod Nimblawar for his Know how, energy and dedication, ventured into the business of rendering Insulation application services under the name of M/s Atharva Insulation & then in 2006 Group has merged into M/s Atharva Engineers with services for installation, Erection, & commissioning of STG set. His dedication and commitment to render quality services and also his benevolent approach and guidance to his engineers and workers has paid rich dividends.

Erection of STG Set small or Medium it be, in addition to swift performance and adherence to schedule encompasses a myriad of services which require a high level of technical expertise and dedication. The customers are therefore always looking for responsible agencies, who possess proven capability and are accountable for their services. ATHARVA Engineers now enjoy the long term and Continuing relationship with the customers like M/s SIEMENS LTD, M/s DRESSER RAND , M/s HINDALCO MAHAN POWER PLANT, M/s DALMIYA CEMENT, M/s. YASH AGRO, M/s. ORIENT GROUP, M/s. RASHMI METALICS & POWER GROUP, M/s. NOVA IRON & STEEL, M/s. SHIVALAYA ISPAT, M/s GAMMON INFRA, M/s IL & FS etc.

Services Offered by us

  • Installation, Erection & Commissioning of New STG Set.
  • Supply of Insulation Materials & Its application for Piping, Heat exchanges, Tanks, Vessels, Heaters.
  • Installation, Erection, termination, Cabling of Supercritical Power plant.
  • Over Hauling, re-rating & Upgrading of Steam Turbine
  • Selection, Evaluation of contracts, consulting services for Customer.
  • Manufacturing of Insulation of Jacket for Steam turbine.
  • Sale Purchase of Old & New Steam Turbines.
