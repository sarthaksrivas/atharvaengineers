Jacket Type Turbine Insulation

Ceramic wool fitted to the shape of turbine casing will be used as an Insulation material. The necessary gaps/cut-outs are made from piping connections, measuring devices etc. in the insulation. Silicon coated cloth and NB cloth have to be sewn together by means of glass yarn to make blankets which will contain the ceramic wool. Insulation mattresses/blankets/jackets must be securely fastened to turbine casing with metal straps at suitable interval before outer cover. For temperature above 400 Degree centigrade, the bottom turbine casing half to be provided with additional third layer of insulation. Insulation material must be asbestos free, also as a whole should not contain internationally banned materials. Insulation thickness calculations need to be done at 30oC ambient temperature. Insulation of the bottom turbine casing half is 30% thicker than the top turbine casing half. Temperature measuring points (for continuous operation and trails) on casing must also not be insulated. The bearing body and the space between the bearing body and the turbine casing must not be blocked by insulation. The accessibility to speed measurement probes must be free and ensure that it is not blocked by insulation.
